🧠 How Our AI Works

Our AI analyzes 10+ years of historical price data, real-time news sentiment, and economic events using machine learning to generate buy/sell/hold signals. The model is retrained weekly using fresh market data collected automatically via Zapier from Yahoo Finance.

The pipeline uses a LightGBM gradient-boosted tree model trained on features including RSI, MACD, exponential moving averages (EMA-20/50), news sentiment scores, CPI delta, and PMI readings. Training data is stored in Google Sheets and the Colab notebook runs on Google's free GPU runtime. Predictions are written back to the Predictions sheet and displayed here in real time.

All tools used are free-tier: Google Sheets · Google Colab · Zapier · Twelve Data · NewsAPI · Google Data Studio. See the Methodology page for full details.
